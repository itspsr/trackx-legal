Privacy Policy – TrackX

Last Updated: January 2025

TrackX (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data.

1. No Personal Data Collection

TrackX does NOT collect or store any personal information such as your email, phone number, name, or address.

2. Location Access

Your location is used ONLY for showing accurate local data such as:

  • Weather
  • AQI
  • Rain Radar
  • Fuel Prices
  • Gold Prices
  • Traffic Map
  • Earthquake info near your region

Your location is never stored or shared.

3. Permissions Used

  • Location: To show live data based on your area.
  • Camera: Only used for the QR Scanner tool.
  • Flashlight: For the Torch tool.

4. Third-Party Data Sources

TrackX uses public APIs for: weather, AQI, radar, gold prices, fuel prices, traffic, earthquakes, and flights/trains.

These requests are anonymous and contain no personal data.

5. Analytics

Anonymous usage analytics may be used to improve app performance. No identity or personal profiling is performed.

6. Security

The app does not store any personal data. All processing happens locally on your device.

7. Contact Us

Email: support.trackx.app@gmail.com

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update the policy occasionally. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
