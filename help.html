Help & Support – TrackX

If you need any help using TrackX, please read the FAQ below or contact support.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why is Weather or AQI not loading?

It may take a few seconds depending on your internet speed. Also ensure location permission is enabled.

2. Rain Radar showing blank?

Radar tiles load slowly on weak connections. Try zooming in or refreshing.

3. Flight / Train shows dummy data?

This may happen when your region's API limit is reached. It will auto-refresh in a few minutes.

4. How do I change my country?

Go to Profile → Change Country → Select your flag.

5. Data seems inaccurate?

TrackX uses multiple public data sources. Accuracy depends on data providers.
